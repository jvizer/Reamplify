How It Works

Transparent, fixed-term mechanics — designed to feel familiar.

🔗

1. Connect Wallet

Connect a Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, Backpack) to view your SOL balance and authorize deposits.

🔒

2. Lock Your SOL

Choose a 3, 6, 9, or 12 month term. Your SOL is locked in a non-custodial vault program for the selected duration.

💵

3. Claim USDC at Maturity

At the unlock date, your original SOL is returned and your fixed USDC yield becomes claimable in a single transaction.
