How It Works

Transparent, fixed-term mechanics — designed to feel familiar.

🔗

1. Connect Wallet

Connect a Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, Backpack). This demo reads your real on-chain SOL and USDT balances via Helius RPC.

🔒

2. Lock Your SOL

Choose a 3, 6, 9, or 12 month term. In production, your SOL would be locked in an audited Solana program; this demo only simulates the flow.

💵

3. Claim USDT at Maturity

At the unlock date, your original SOL is returned and your fixed USDT yield becomes claimable. This demo does not send real funds.

Important: This page is a front-end template. Wallet connection and balances are live via Helius RPC, but no smart contract is deployed — the deposit, lock, and yield payout are simulations only and no real funds are moved. Any production version must disclose the yield source, counterparty, custody model, smart contract audits, redemption mechanics, and applicable regulatory status. Fixed yields on locked crypto deposits carry significant risk, including principal loss. Never deposit real funds into a vault that has not been independently audited.
